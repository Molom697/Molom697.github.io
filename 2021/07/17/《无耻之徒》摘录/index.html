  • That’s how horny and lonely I’ve been these days, like a dog humping a lamppost.

  • What the hell is going on? I don’t want you as my friend. I want you as my boyfriend. Look at me! I’m nice, smart, funny, and I’d fuck up anyone that hurt you. I’m sick of having to change, who I am or wait ‘till I’m older or–or rape someone into being with me. I like you, and I’d be a good girlfriend. And if you can’t see that, screw you!

08

  • 你得清楚自己想要什么，每天战战兢兢，觉得很内疚，这不算决定，你得做出决定。你很好斗，菲奥娜，你知道如何斗争，不用假装自己不会。所以要么为这段感情斗争，要么赶紧放他走，让他平复创伤，开始下一段感情。

And I don’t even know if I really know what love is, you know, except a thing that I fuck up all the time. But you, I respect the shit out of you. And I miss you. I just keep wishing you were with me when everything came. *
